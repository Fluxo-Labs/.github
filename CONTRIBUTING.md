# Contributing to Fluxo Labs Organization

Thank you for your interest in helping Fluxo Labs grow! This guide outlines how you can contribute to the **organizational and strategic** side of the collective.

## How to Join the Core Team
Our organization thrives on merit and consistent collaboration. Here is how you can step up:

### 1. Engage in Discussions
The first step is active participation in our **GitHub Discussions**. Help us refine architectural specs, answer user questions, or propose new initiatives.

### 2. Organizational Contributions
You don't need to write Rust to help us lead:
- **Issue Triage:** Help us categorize reports, reproduce bugs, and keep our backlog clean.
- **Governance:** Assist in defining our contribution workflows and community standards.
- **Documentation:** Improve the clarity of our architectural guides and READMEs.

### 3. Becoming a Member
Membership (write access and decision-making power) is granted based on:
- **Consistency:** Regular contributions (code, management, or docs) over time.
- **Ownership:** Taking initiative on specific tasks or sub-projects.
- **Proposal:** If you feel ready to take a structural role, open an issue titled "Proposal: Core Membership" and tell us how you'd like to help.

## Code of Conduct
We are committed to a respectful and inclusive environment. All members are expected to uphold professional collaboration standards.

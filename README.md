# 🌊 Fluxo Labs

> Fluxo Labs is an open-source collective dedicated to crafting high-performance, zero-cost architectural primitives for the Rust ecosystem. 

We believe that complex design patterns shouldn't be a burden, but a compile-time guarantee. Our mission is to bridge the gap between abstract architecture and safe, efficient code.

## 🤝 Beyond Code: Help us Organize
We are looking for passionate individuals to help us scale the organization. If you want to contribute to the **management and growth** of Fluxo Labs, we are looking for:

- **Project Managers:** To define roadmaps and coordinate releases across repositories.
- **Moderators:** To foster a healthy community and manage discussions.
- **Communicators & Tech Writers:** To document our architecture and share our progress.
- **Community Leads:** To help onboard new contributors and manage outreach.

Interested in shaping the future of Fluxo? Read our [Contributing Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md) or start a thread in our **Discussions** tab!
